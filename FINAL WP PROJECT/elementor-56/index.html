Three Core Crises

What keeps families from moving forward

📚
Educational Abandonment
Over 300,000 children drop out of school each year — most because families cannot afford supplies, transport or meals. Without education, generational poverty is unavoidable.
🍞
Chronic Food Insecurity
In Hay Mohammadi, Derb Sultan and Sidi Moumen, families skip meals systematically. Malnutrition in the first 5 years causes irreversible developmental damage.
🏥
Absent Healthcare
Preventable diseases go untreated because a consultation costs more than a day's income. Our mobile units bring the clinic to the community — not the other way around.
❤️ Real Impact Story
Amina's Journey

She almost gave up
on school forever.

📍 Hay Mohammadi, Casablanca · Age 12

✕
Before — September 2022
Amina's father lost his factory job. With four siblings and a sick grandmother to care for, she dropped out of Grade 4 to sell vegetables at the market every day.
🫶
Our Intervention — February 2023
Our social workers found Amina through our neighborhood outreach program. Within two weeks she received full school supplies, daily meal vouchers, weekly tutoring and a monthly family food basket.
★
Today — May 2025
Amina is in Grade 6. She placed 3rd in her school's maths competition. Her mother joined our women's skills program. Her brother enrolled in our nutrition program. One family — three lives changed.

"Before, I thought school was for other children — the ones with new shoes and full lunchboxes. Now I know I belong there too. I want to become an engineer and build water systems for villages like my grandmother's."

— Amina, 12, Hay Mohammadi, Casablanca
